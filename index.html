Frequently Asked Questions

What is Buffy's middle name in Buffy the Vampire Slayer?

Buffy's middle name is Anne. Fun fact: in the premiere of season 3, after Buffy has run away, she takes up a new identity and calls herself "Anne".

How many love interests did Buffy have throughout the show?

Buffy had three primary romances during the course of the show. Angel, Spike, and Riley Finn.

What are Spike and Angel's real names?

Angel's real name, as revealed in a flashback, is Liam. Spike, whose real name was also given in a flashback episode, is William.

Which character was the first to be cast in their role?

Rupert Giles, played by Anthony Stewart, was the first person that got cast on the very day that the casting directors began looking at actors.
